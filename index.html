👋 WELCOME TO FAMILYBOOK

This is a place we can share our old photos, thoughts, and things we otherwise don't want the rest of the world to see or hear.

Before you create your profile, here is a quick guide:

1️⃣ Use Your First Name
We all know each other here! Just use your first name so the wall stays clean and easy to read.
2️⃣ Complete Your Setup
Once logged in, click the ⚙️ Gear Icon next to your name. Add your birthday, cell phone, and pick an app color theme!
3️⃣ Install as an App
Click the ℹ️ APP SETUP link on the wall to add this site to your phone's home screen. It will look and act like a real app!
4️⃣ Turn On Alerts
Click the 🔔 SET-UP ALERTS link to join our private Telegram group for instant group texts and notifications.
5️⃣ The Event Menu
Click the 🍔 EVENT button at the top to sign up for upcoming potlucks, or remove items you no longer need.
6️⃣ The Arcade
Click the 🎲 ARCADE tab to play family trivia, or jump into the live 3-day Guess the Baby photo challenge!
